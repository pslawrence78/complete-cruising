Cruise logistics

Naples is one of the stronger independent exploration ports because the city centre is close to the cruise area. Longer trips to Pompeii, Herculaneum or the Amalfi Coast require stronger time discipline.

⭐

Top highlights

PompeiiWorld-famous Roman site. High value, higher effort.
HerculaneumCompact Roman alternative with strong history value.
Historic centreLower effort city immersion and flexible pacing.
Castel dell'OvoHarbour views, sea air and photo potential.
Galleria Umberto IElegant architecture and easy city stop.
Pizza stopLocal experience with strong family appeal.
👨‍👩‍👦

Family lens

Best balance: stay central, keep walking manageable, build in shade, and make pizza part of the experience.

Seb angle: Vesuvius, Pompeii and Roman history are likely to land well if framed as a geography and history challenge.
